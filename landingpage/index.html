How it works

Three steps to take control.

ScreenRot uses Apple's Screen Time API to track and manage your usage — no data leaves your device.

Grant Screen Time access

Open the app and tap "Get Started." ScreenRot needs Screen Time permission to see your usage and block apps on your behalf.

Meet your kraken

Your kraken reacts to your daily screen time in real-time. Check the Overview tab to see your stats, app breakdown, and your kraken's current mood.

Set up your shields

Add usage limits, schedule block routines, or quick-block apps. The Shield tab is your control center — the kraken handles the rest.
